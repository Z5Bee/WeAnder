Take the Long Way Home

Explore More Than the Fastest Route

Most navigation apps prioritize speed and efficiency, often reducing travel to the fastest possible route. While effective, this approach removes the opportunity for discovery, overlooks local businesses, and contributes to stressful, utility-driven experiences. Travel becomes transactional rather than meaningful. There is a need for a navigation experience that values the journey itself. An app that encourages users to slow down, explore scenic routes, and discover the small, local places that make travel memorable.

About WeAnder

WeAnder is a navigation app designed to guide users along scenic routes while highlighting local businesses, landmarks, and unexpected stops along the way. Rather than optimizing only for speed, WeAnder prioritizes the while experience. The app suggests alternative routes that are visually interesting and integrates discovery into navigation through real-time recommendations for small businesses, scenic views, and cultural landmarks. By combining navigation with exploration, WeAnder transforms everyday travel into a more thoughtful and engaging experience.
